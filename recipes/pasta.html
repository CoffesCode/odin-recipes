Pasta

I personally consider pasta to be the best food in the world, but of course, that's just my opinion.

Ingredients

  • 500 g screw or fidelinho pasta
  • 2 liters of water
  • 1 spoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of oil
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 chopped onion
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Put the water to boil
  2. When it is boiling, add the salt and 1 tablespoon of oil.
  3. When the pasta is soft, remove it from the heat and drain the water in the pasta colander.
  4. Wash the pasta.
  5. Make the sauce of your choice in another pan.
  6. Place the margarine, tomato, onion and pepper in a pan
  7. Saute and add a spoonful of salt.
  8. Add the pasta.
  9. If you want, you can add tomato paste.
  10. Mix and serve with grated cheese.
